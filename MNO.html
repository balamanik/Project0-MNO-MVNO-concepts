Concept of Mobile Network Operator

as we have seen in the Own House page, we are going to explore the similarities of MNO and own house.

    MNO has the followings:

  • MNO would have own network infrastructure.

  • MNO will have their own HLR (Home Location Register) which contains subscriber details including location information.

  • MNO will have full control over their network as well as the subscribers.

  • MNO could be act as an Mobile virtual network enabler(MVNE) to offer virtual network operations either full or ligh MVNO networks. Please see the MVNO page for further details
