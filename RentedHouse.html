Rented House

as we have seen in the Own House page, we are going to explore the similarities of Rented house to explain the Full MVNO concept.

    If you have a Rented house:

  • you won't have ownership of the house but renting it out for a rent payment.

  • your name will not be registered with land rigistry or similar governmental body against the property.

  • you cann't alter, sell or rent your full or partial house.

  • you pay council or local authority taxes and charges as like an owner of a house,for regular waste collection, maintanance of your roads, parks and other amenities etc..

  • you can invite friends and families at anytime but you may have to inform land loard.
