Rented Room

Rent a room is a completely different concept compare to own house or rented house concepts.Renting a room concept brings another aspect of MVNO called light MVNO.

    If you have rented a room:

  • you won't have any sort of ownership in that house.

  • Your freedom is limited within the room and you might have limited access to bathroom, living room etc.

  • you pay a rent.

  • you won't pay any council tax or charges

  • most of the time,you cann't invite friends and families.

  • you might have given a copy of a front door key. you will be however asked to come home before certain hours.
